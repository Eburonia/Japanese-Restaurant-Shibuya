About our Restaurant

Please feel welcome at Japanese Restaurant Shibuya in Maastricht.

The Nakamoto family warmly invites you to dine at our restaurant and experience Japanese cuisine at its finest.

As a family-owned restaurant, Shibuya instantly established a superior name for itself when it opened in 2000.

After 21 years in business, we still serve the best Japanese food in the area.

Our restaurant specializes in authentic Japanese cuisine. Try our delicious Sushi and Sashimi dishes prepared by Satoshi Nakamoto and his team.

At Japanese Restaurant Shibuya, we honor Japanese cuisine by selecting only the best high-quality and fresh ingrediënts.

Are you looking for a night-out dining experience? Make a reservation and enjoy the authentic Japanese cuisine at Japanese Restaurant Shibuya.

We are located in the city center of Maastricht.

In case of questions or comments, please feel free to fill in the contact form for more information.
