About our Restaurant

Welcome to Japanese Restaurant Shibuya!

After 21 years in business, we still serve the best Japanese food in the Maastricht area.

Shibuya specializes in authentic Japanese cuisine — try our delicious Sushi and Sashimi dishes prepared by the owner and his team.

A family-owned restaurant, Shibuya instantly established a superior name for itself when it opened in 2000.

Owner Satoshi Nakamoto continues the rich Japanese tradition begun 21 years ago by his Japanese immigrant parents.

His wife Masako and their two sons also devote their time to help manage the business.

Nakamoto family warmly invites you to dine at Shibuya and experience Japanese cuisine at its finest.

If you prefer to dine at home, we offer a full take-out menu for pick-up or delivery.
